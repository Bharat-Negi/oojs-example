
undefined vs null Comparison:-

+---------------------------+----------------------------------------+-------------------------------------+
| Aspect                    | undefined                              | null                                |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------------+-------------------------------------+
| Meaning                   | Variable exists but no value assigned  | Intentional empty value             |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------------+-------------------------------------+
| Type                      | "undefined"                            | "object"                            |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------------+-------------------------------------+
| Assignment                | Automatic (by JavaScript)              | Manual (set by developer)           |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------------+-------------------------------------+
| Use Case                  | Missing or uninitialized values        | Clearing or resetting variables     |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------------+-------------------------------------+
| Equality (==)             | true                                   | true                                |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------------+-------------------------------------+
| Strict Equality (===)     | false                                  | false                               |
+---------------------------+----------------------------------------+-------------------------------------+
