HTML `<b>` vs `<strong>` Explained:-

Side-by-Side Comparison

Feature          <strong>                          <b>
Type             Semantic (adds meaning)          Presentational (adds style)

Purpose          Indicates importance,            Stylistically offsets text without
                 seriousness, or urgency          added importance

Default Look     Bold                             Bold

Accessibility    Screen readers announce it       Screen readers do not change
                 with emphasis (different tone)   their tone. Read normally.

SEO              Can be used by search engines    Treated as pure styling choice,
                 to understand key content        no semantic weight

Best For         Warnings, critical information,  Product names, keywords,
                 key points                       lead sentences, visual highlights
